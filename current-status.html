Current Status

Palestine, and New York City has been making efforts to address gender inequality in employment in recent years. However, gender disparities in the job market persist.

Palestine

Female Labor Force Participation: In 2020, the female labor force participation rate in
Palestine was only 18.4%, compared to 68.6% for men.

A World Bank report released in September 2020 indicated that the unemployment rate among women in Palestine rose to approximately 38%, a figure that far exceeds the unemployment rate among men, which reached about 23% during the same period.

New York

In New York State, the ratio of women to men in jobs and careers that offer high earning potential, including jobs traditionally dominated by men, is 41.4% for 2016-2020. This compares favorably to the 37.5% rate for the period considered in last yearâ€™s report (2015-2019).

Women hold only 28% of STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) jobs in the US, despite accounting for nearly half of the total US workforce.

Palestine

Unemployment: Women in Palestine also face higher unemployment rates compared to men. In the first quarter of 2021, the unemployment rate for women was 50.4%, compared to 29.7% for men.

Data from the Palestinian Bureau of Economic Analysis indicates that the unemployment rate among women in Palestine is about 42.6%, which is much higher than the unemployment rate among men which is about 18.9%. This rate is increasing in urban sectors and in Gaza in particular, where unemployment rates for women are high.

New York

According to data from the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics, as of 2020, the employment rate for women in New York was 53.6%, which is slightly lower than the national average for women in the United States at 54.1%.

Women hold just 6.6% of CEO positions at Fortune 500 companies, and are underrepresented in fields such as technology and engineering.
